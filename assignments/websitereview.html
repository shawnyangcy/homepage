The site try to appeal to the target market BY

The site try to appeal to the target market BY

Simple, navigable web page.

Because all the shoppers like to simply buy the things that they like, without too much advertisement or irrelevant information.

Introducing their new product feature just by using a maximum of 10 words.

With great picture of product with simple tones and backgrounds,. Although every product have their advantages, but the problem is how to explain the advantage with simple words, and conclude all the brand new features. A simple introduction will let shoppers more interesting on product.

Animations and pictures

A lot of companies don't care about the animations and pictures on their website about their product, but most shoppers care about is the appearance of the product. A simple, beautiful animation, picture or video will leave a strong first impression.
